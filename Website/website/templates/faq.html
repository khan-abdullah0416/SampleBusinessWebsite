    Have a question?


    What products do you specialise in?

    We are a company that specialises in making smart home devices, such as, virtual assistant technology. This helps to automate redundant tasks, like making a to-do list. Please check our features page for more information.

    How do I make an account?

    Click the sign up button on the top right hand corner of the screen. You need to enter an email, your first name, a password and what type of account you would like, as well as other information.

    What does the profile page show?

    Your profile displays your name and your email address. Once linked with one of our smart home devices you can access the voice logs and send inputs from your online account.

    What does your voice assistant do?

    The embedded system takes in voice data and relays an output either in the form of a voice response or changes a value in one of your appliances. Watch a video on Google Nest to compare it our system.
