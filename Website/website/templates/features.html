    Introducing, SHS Hub, your virtual assistant

    January 14, 2022 by Management

    This is Smart Home Solution's first home automation system. Use this device to get the most control of your home with voice inputs and outputs. This device strives from simplicity, giving you the most essential features of a virtual assistant. This system helps to complete daily tasks quickly and efficiently.

    Our expert engineers developed the code so that your voice recognition device will always be working. There are two modes available, offline and online. This device will enable you to work efficiently and easily.

    Keep track of time

    Keep specific track of time by calling "What time is it?". The system outputs the time with the date, hour and second as well! 

    speak(ctime())

    Need to find a location?

    Too tired to type an address? By calling the key word "location" then followed up by the address, our system performs a quick Google search to find the location.

    1. First, ask the virtual system to search the address.
    2. Then, they will ask what is location of the place.
    3. Reply with the address and the output appears on screen.

    Don't worry, our system doesn't keep track of what you search!

    New Search Feature and a To-Do List

    February 3, 2022 by Engineers

    Search with your voice

    The location feature has been extended to allow a quick Google search on anything!

    This feature does not allow Smart Home Solutions to keep track of your search history. A Google account may be required to access some services.

    Keep track of your daily tasks with a to-do list

    A to-do list feature has been introduced into the system. Create, add and delete your to-do list with only your voice. Your virtual assistant can help you keep track of what you need to do.

    Link your system with your online account

    March 11, 2022 by Management

    Create an account on our website to access your smart home device online. On your Profile page you will see the voice logs of your virtual assistant. You are also able to see your to-do list. In the event that the system does not pick up your voice, you can send a command to your system from the Profile page.

    This feature does allow Smart Home Solutions to keep track of your voice log history with your system. This helps us keep track of what features and update are needed. An email account may be required to access some services.
